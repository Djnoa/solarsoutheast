The Process of Going Solar

Step one: Initial Consultation

Stepping into the world of solar energy starts with an initial consultation with our experienced team at Solar South East. In this essential first step, we engage in a thorough discussion about your current energy usage, aspirations for switching to solar, and any questions or concerns you might have.

We're here to demystify solar energy, explaining how it can benefit your household or business, both environmentally and financially. We'll provide an honest, straightforward outlook on what you can expect when transitioning to solar, considering factors such as your property's location, roof size, orientation, and potential shading issues.

Step two: Site Survey & Custom Design

Once you decide to proceed, the next step is a comprehensive site survey. Our skilled survey team will visit your property at a time that suits you to conduct a thorough evaluation. They'll assess your roof's condition, the availability of sunlight, potential obstructions, and other specific factors critical to the successful operation of a solar power system.

Post survey, our expert engineers utilize the data collected to design a bespoke solar solution tailored to your property and energy needs. Every detail, from the optimal positioning of each panel to the best-suited equipment, is carefully considered to ensure maximum solar generation. By aligning our design to your unique circumstances, we guarantee an efficient, high-performing solar system that seamlessly blends with your property aesthetics.

Step three: Installation

When the design is approved, our expert installers come to your property, equipped to implement your customized solar solution. We install the solar panels, wire them to your electric system, and ensure all components are secure and operating at their peak. Our team strictly adheres to safety standards, ensuring the protection of your property and its occupants.

Post-installation, we conduct rigorous testing and familiarize you with your new system's operation. Through a real-time monitoring process, we ensure you stay updated on your system's performance and any maintenance needs. Our commitment at Solar South East extends beyond providing solar solutions - we ensure a superior service experience from installation onwards.
